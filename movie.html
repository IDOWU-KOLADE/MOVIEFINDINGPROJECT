About FlickFind

🎬 FlickFind — Movie Discovery, Reimagined

FlickFind is a modern movie discovery platform built to eliminate endless scrolling and indecision. Instead of wasting time jumping between apps, FlickFind gives you a fast, clean, and intelligent way to search, explore, and save movies — all in one place.

Whether you already know what you're looking for or just want something that matches your mood, FlickFind helps you get there instantly.

🚀 Core Features

  • 🔍 Smart Search System
    Instantly find movies by typing just a few characters. Results update dynamically and load smoothly without refreshing the page.
  • 🎭 Mood-Based Discovery
    Explore movies based on vibes like Happy, Sad, Action, or Mind-Blowing. Perfect for when you don’t know what to watch.
  • ❤️ Personal Wishlist
    Save movies you love and access them anytime. Your wishlist is stored locally, so it stays even after refresh.
  • 🎬 Detailed Movie Modal
    Click any movie to view full details including plot, cast, rating, runtime, and more — without leaving the page.
  • 🌗 Dark & Light Mode
    Switch between themes for comfort and style. Your preference is saved automatically.
  • 📱 Fully Responsive Design
    Optimized for both mobile and desktop — smooth experience everywhere.
  • 📌 Load More System
    Seamlessly fetch more results without interrupting your browsing flow.

🧠 How to Use FlickFind

  • Start typing in the search bar to instantly see results.
  • Click any movie card to open detailed information.
  • Use the ❤️ icon to add/remove movies from your wishlist.
  • Tap mood buttons to quickly explore curated search ideas.
  • Use the navigation (sidebar or top bar) to switch between sections.

⚙️ Under the Hood

  • HTML5 — Structured layout
  • CSS3 — Styling, animations, and responsiveness
  • Vanilla JavaScript — Core logic and interactivity
  • OMDb API — Real-time movie data
  • LocalStorage — Persistent wishlist and theme settings
